The Path to Connection: Our Outreach Plan

Our outreach is a strategic, three-step process designed to build genuine connections. This is not about spamming, but about identifying the right people and starting valuable conversations.

1

Identify Companies

Use targeted Google searches and industry lists (e.g., "fastest-growing companies in Toronto") to build a highly relevant list of potential clients.

2

Find the Right Person

Navigate to the company's LinkedIn page and use the "People" tab to find decision-makers with titles like "Marketing Director" or "Head of Communications".

3

Connect Strategically

Prioritize leveraging mutual connections for a warm introduction. If none exist, send a personalized connection request that clearly states your value and purpose.
